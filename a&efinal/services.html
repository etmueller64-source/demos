Every engagement moves through the same three layers - so you always know what's happening, what's next, and what you're getting.

Layer 1.0

Design

What's included

  • Discovery call - your business, your customers, your goals
  • Custom visual identity that fits your brand
  • Mobile-first layout, designed screen by screen
  • A clickable preview you approve before any build

Why it matters

Templates make every business look like every other business. We design for yours specifically - the way your customers find you, judge you, and decide to call you. Nothing goes to build until you've clicked through it and love it.

Layer 2.0

Build

What's included

  • Hand-coded, fast-loading pages - no page builders
  • Click-to-call, booking & contact forms, maps
  • Live data feeds (schedules, rosters, menus) from Google Sheets
  • Hosting, domain & SSL set up properly

Why it matters

Speed and reliability aren't features - they're the baseline. Hand-built sites load faster, rank better, and never break because a plugin updated. And live data means your site updates itself when your spreadsheet does.

Layer 3.0

Grow

What's included

  • Content updates as your business changes
  • Live Google rating & reviews on your site
  • Search visibility basics done right
  • A real person to call when you need a change

Why it matters

A website isn't finished at launch - it's finished never. Seasons change, prices change, staff change. We keep your site current so it keeps working as hard on day 500 as it did on day one.
