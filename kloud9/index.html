Why Wagtail

Real care, from real people who love dogs

Wagtail started as a neighbourhood favourite in Newmarket and has grown into a trusted home-away-from-home for local dogs — the kind of place where the same familiar faces greet your pup at drop-off and pick-up, every time.

Because we're open around the clock, your dog's routine never has to pause for yours. Whether it's an overnight stay, a daytime drop-in, or ongoing training, every visit is planned around your dog's personality, not a one-size-fits-all schedule.
